Crispy Nuggets


Prep time

Cook Time

Serves

Ingredients

Boneless Chicken Breast 250gm

Soya Sauce 1 tbsp

Salt ½ tsp

Garlic Paste ½ tsp

Ginger Paste ¼ tsp

Lemon Juice ½

Black Pepper Powder 1 tsp

Red Chili Powder ½ tsp

Yogurt 1 tbsp

Breadcrumbs 1-2 cups

Egg 1

Milk 1 tsp

Salt & Pepper a pinch

Oil for deep frying

Buy These Ingredients Now

Cooking Directions

  1. Marinate chicken cubes with above ingredients for an hour.
  2. Just before frying, beat one egg with a tsp, of milk.
  3. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. In a separate plate put some breadcrumbs.
  5. Now dip the chicken cubes first in the egg & milk mixture, then roll in the breadcrumbs.
  6. Fry over medium heat until golden in color.
  7. Remove and drain any excess oil.
